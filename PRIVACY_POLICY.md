# Privacy Policy - Expense Tracker App

**Last Updated: December 3, 2025**

## 1. Overview

Expense Tracker ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Guest Mode

When using the app in Guest Mode:

- **No Personal Data Collected** - Guest users can use the app without providing any personal information
- **Local Storage Only** - All expenses are stored locally on your device
- **No Account Required** - No email, password, or identification is needed

### 2.2 Authenticated Mode (When Backend Available)

When using authenticated features:

- **Email & Password** - Collected only to create/access your account
- **User Profile** - Name and account information
- **Expense Data** - Your expense records including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions

### 2.3 Device Information

- **Device Identifiers** - App may collect anonymous device data for crash reporting
- **Usage Analytics** - Anonymous usage patterns to improve the app
- **Network Status** - Your connection status is checked locally only

## 3. How We Use Your Information

- To provide and maintain app functionality
- To sync your data (when backend is available)
- To improve app performance and user experience
- To fix bugs and technical issues
- To send important notifications about your expenses (when authenticated)

## 4. Data Storage

### 4.1 Guest Mode

- All data is stored **only on your device** using local storage
- **No data is sent to our servers**
- Data persists until you explicitly clear the app

### 4.2 Authenticated Mode

- Data may be synced to our secure servers
- Data is encrypted during transmission
- Accounts can be deleted, which removes all associated data

## 5. Data Security

- We implement industry-standard security measures
- Guest mode data never leaves your device
- Authentication uses secure token-based sessions
- We do not store passwords in plain text

## 6. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party libraries:

- **Expo** - App platform and services
- **AsyncStorage** - Local device storage
- **Axios** - Network requests (authenticated mode only)

These services have their own privacy policies that may apply.

## 7. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13 years old. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date above.

## 9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

**Email:** support@expensetracker.com  
**In-App:** Use the Settings menu to report privacy concerns

## 10. User Rights

- **Right to Access:** You can access and review your data
- **Right to Delete:** You can delete your account and all associated data
- **Right to Control:** Guest users have complete control over their local data
